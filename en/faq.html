Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about What to Eat service.

Is the service free?

Yes, all features of What to Eat are completely free. You can use it right away without any registration.

What types of food are recommended?

We offer popular dishes from various categories including Korean, Chinese, Japanese, Western, and street food. Our menu includes over 16 items such as Kimchi Stew, Soybean Paste Stew, Bibimbap, Samgyeopsal, Fried Chicken, Pizza, Hamburger, Pasta, Sushi, Ramen, Jajangmyeon, and Tteokbokki.

Can I save or share the results?

Yes! You can save your results as an image or share them on social media. Share the fun of choosing your menu with friends.

Does it work on mobile?

Absolutely! What to Eat is built with responsive web design, so you can use it conveniently on smartphones, tablets, PCs, and all other devices.

How does Food World Cup work?

16 foods compete in a tournament bracket format. Each round, you choose the food you want to eat more between two options. The final winner becomes today's recommended meal!

What is Quick Pick?

Quick Pick is a feature that recommends a random food with just one button click. Use it when you're short on time or want to leave it completely to chance. You'll get your result in about 5 seconds.

What is Food Tarot?

Food Tarot is a fun feature where you select one of 5 tarot cards and receive a food recommendation along with today's food fortune. It takes about 30 seconds.

How do I play Balance Game?

Balance Game helps you discover your food preferences through 5 extreme either-or questions. For example, you'll answer questions like 'Spicy food vs. Mild food' to get your final recommendation.

What is Payment King?

Payment King is a fun feature to decide who pays for the meal. It offers two methods: Spin the Roulette and Ladder Game. Simply enter the participants' names and it will fairly select who pays. It takes about 30 seconds.
