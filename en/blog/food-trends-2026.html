5 Food Trends to Watch in 2026

January 12, 2026 - 5 min read

What Will We Be Eating in 2026?

Food trends reflect the changes in our society. Health consciousness, environmental concerns, globalization, and technological advances are all shaping what ends up on our plates. Let's explore the top food trends to watch in 2026.

1. The Evolution of Hybrid Korean Cuisine

'Hybrid Korean food' - traditional Korean dishes fused with global flavors - continues to evolve. From kimchi pasta to bulgogi tacos to gochujang burgers, Korean flavors are being reimagined in exciting modern ways.

  • Fusion street food following the success of rose tteokbokki
  • Global applications of Korean sauces (gochujang, ssamjang)
  • Expansion of Korean fine dining

2. Sustainable Eating

As environmental awareness grows, so does the interest in sustainable food options. Low carbon footprint ingredients, local produce, and plant-based proteins are gaining attention.

  • Evolution of plant-based meats - tastier and more diverse products
  • Emphasis on local food and seasonal ingredients
  • Food upcycling - products made from would-be waste ingredients
  • Carbon labeling - displaying the environmental impact of food

3. Functional Foods and Personalized Nutrition

Beyond just filling our stomachs, foods with enhanced health benefits are gaining popularity. Personalized diets based on individual health conditions and goals are also expanding.

  • Probiotic foods for gut health
  • Immunity-boosting foods
  • Expansion of low-sugar and low-carb options
  • DNA-based personalized nutrition supplements

4. The Expansion of Asian Fusion

Along with the global rise of K-food, interest in other Asian cuisines is growing. Southeast Asian food, in particular, is emerging as a new trend.

  • Continued popularity of Vietnamese food (pho, banh mi)
  • Growing interest in Thai cuisine
  • New discoveries in Filipino and Malaysian food
  • Asian street food concepts

5. The Polarization of Convenience and Experience

The market is simultaneously growing in two directions: convenient meal solutions for busy modern life, and premium dining experiences that offer something special.

  • Evolution of premium meal kits - restaurant-quality at home
  • Healthy convenience store meals
  • Experiential restaurants - chef's tables, cooking classes
  • Pop-up restaurants and gourmet events

What's on Your Table in 2026?

You don't have to follow every trend, but trying new flavors can be a great adventure. Why not challenge yourself to try something new this year?

After all, the best food is the food you love. Regardless of trends, enjoy a delicious meal today!
