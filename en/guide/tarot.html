What is Food Tarot?

Food Tarot is a mystical recommendation method where you draw a tarot card to reveal your food fortune for the day. Select one of the face-down cards, and as it flips over, your matching food and fortune message are revealed.

Beyond simple food recommendations, you receive a message with advice and energy for the day, creating a special experience. Draw a card with the same excitement as visiting a fortune teller.

Food-Specific Tarot Messages

Each food has a unique tarot message. Through meaningful messages connected to each food's characteristics, you can gain small inspirations for your day:

  • Pork Belly - "Time to recharge! Regain your vitality with a hearty meal"
  • Bibimbap - "Various opportunities are mixing in. Harmony is your destiny today"
  • Sushi - "A refined mindset brings luck. A clean choice is the answer!"
  • Malatang - "Time for burning passion! Bold challenges lead to success"

Pizza, chicken, tonkatsu, and many more foods also have special messages prepared.

The Secret to Card Selection

"Which card should I choose?" There's no right answer. The essence of tarot is intuition. When you see the three cards, choose the one your eyes go to first, or where your hand naturally moves.

Don't overthink it. Tarot is about trusting your momentary intuition. Whichever card you choose, that's the message you need today.

FAQ

Q. Is the fortune message real?
A. Food Tarot is for fun and inspiration. It's different from actual divination, but positive messages can help start your day in a good mood.

Q. The same card keeps appearing.
A. Maybe that food is specially calling to you today! But if you want different results, just press "Draw Again."

Q. What if I don't like the food that came up?
A. Embracing the tarot's wisdom is good, but you can always draw again if you really don't want it. Though sometimes you discover new joys in foods you usually don't eat!
