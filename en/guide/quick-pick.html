What is Quick Pick?

Quick Pick is the fastest and simplest food recommendation feature offered by What to Eat. With just one button click, you can get a random recommendation from 16 popular menu items. Just as the name suggests, your menu for today is decided in literally one second.

When lunch time is approaching and you just can't decide what to eat, when you don't want to waste time choosing a menu with friends, or when you just want to leave it up to fate - try Quick Pick. You'll get an answer immediately without any complicated process.

What is Decision Fatigue?

Decision fatigue is a phenomenon where judgment and willpower deteriorate due to repetitive decision-making. This is why Steve Jobs wore the same clothes every day, and why Mark Zuckerberg sticks to gray t-shirts. They didn't want to waste energy on trivial decisions.

The same applies to choosing meals. You need to make 3 meal decisions a day, which adds up to 21 decisions a week. When these decisions accumulate, the energy you need for important work or creative activities gets depleted. Quick Pick takes care of these small but tiring decisions for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What if I don't like the recommendation?
A. You can press "Try Another" button to get unlimited new recommendations. However, for the true Quick Pick experience, we recommend trusting and following the first result!

Q. What if a food I'm allergic to comes up?
A. If you have allergies to certain foods, just use the retry button when that food appears. We plan to add an exclusion setting feature in future updates.

Q. The same food keeps coming up.
A. Since it's completely random, the same food can appear consecutively. Think of it as fate and embrace it! üòÑ
