Food World Cup - Tournament Game

The food that survives the 16-team tournament becomes your true menu of the day!

What is Food World Cup?

Food World Cup is a game that pits 16 popular foods against each other in tournament style to determine the ultimate winner. Each round, you choose which of two foods appeals to you more, and the winner advances to the next round. Through Round of 16 ‚Üí Quarterfinals ‚Üí Semifinals ‚Üí Finals, your champion food is decided.

Unlike random selection, this method reflects your preferences at every moment, resulting in higher satisfaction with the result. You'll have that "Aha! This is what I really wanted to eat today!" moment.

How to Play

  1. Click the üèÜ Food World Cup card on the What to Eat main screen.
  2. When the Round of 16 starts, two foods appear on screen.
  3. Click the food you'd rather eat - that food advances to the next round.
  4. After 15 total selections, your champion food is decided!
  5. Share your result or challenge again.

Why Tournament Style?

Ranking things is hard. If asked to rank all 16 foods, you'd feel overwhelmed. But if asked to choose between Kimchi Stew vs Jajangmyeon? Much easier.

Tournament style transforms complex choices into a series of simple binary choices. Each individual choice is easy, but together they find what you want most. This is the magic of Food World Cup.

FAQ

Q. What if I want to eat both?
A. You still have to choose one! Think about which you'd pick if you had to eat right now. That instant intuition decides today's menu.

Q. The same food always wins.
A. That's your ultimate favorite! But sometimes it's good to try 2nd or 3rd place too. Click "Try Again" on the result screen to find a different winner.

Q. Can I quit mid-game?
A. Yes, click "Back" to return to the main screen anytime. However, progress isn't saved, so you'll start from Round of 16 next time.
