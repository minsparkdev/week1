What is Balance Game?

Balance Game analyzes your food preferences through 10 food-related this-or-that questions and recommends the menu that best matches you. As you answer eternal dilemmas like "Black Bean Noodles vs Spicy Seafood Noodles" or "Original Fried vs Seasoned Chicken," your food personality gradually reveals itself.

This isn't simple random recommendation. AI analyzes your choice patterns to calculate 5 taste indicators (spicy, hearty, adventurous, social, quick), then recommends the food with the highest matching score among 16 options.

Recommendation Algorithm

Balance Game uses a scientific matching algorithm, not simple score tallying. Each food has trait values for 5 indicators (e.g., Malatang = spicy 5, adventurous 4), and it finds the food most similar to the taste profile formed by your choices.

Thanks to this method, answering differently results in completely different recommendations. Get personalized picks that reflect your mood and condition today.

FAQ

Q. What if I dislike both options?
A. Choose the "lesser evil." The essence of Balance Game is extreme choices. Your preferences show even when picking second-best.

Q. I don't like my result.
A. Try playing again! Question order is random, and you might make different choices based on today's mood, leading to different results.

Q. Is the taste analysis accurate?
A. Perfect analysis from 10 questions is difficult, but most people say "Oh, that's right!" It's more fun when you compare results with friends.
