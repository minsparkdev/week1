About Our Service

What to Eat is a free food recommendation service created to answer the daily question "What to eat today?" From lunch decisions to dinner dilemmas, we provide fun and diverse menu recommendations for everyone who struggles with food choices.

We understand that modern people have to make dozens of big and small decisions every day. Among them, choosing what to eat actually consumes more mental energy than you might think. There's even a psychological term called "Decision Fatigue" - repeated choices wear us down.

What to Eat was created to reduce this decision fatigue. From simple random recommendations to game-style selections, we help you decide today's menu in various ways that suit your situation and mood.

Our Philosophy

What to Eat values the following principles:

  • Simplicity - Anyone can use it immediately without signing up. No app installation required.
  • Fun - Beyond simple recommendations, we provide experiences you can enjoy like a game.
  • Practicality - We recommend realistic menus that you can actually eat.
  • Free - All features are provided free of charge. No hidden costs.

FAQ

Q. Do I need to sign up?
No, What to Eat can be used immediately without any registration. Simply visit our website and start using the menu recommendation service right away.

Q. What kinds of food are recommended?
Our database includes 16 popular foods that are easily accessible in Korea, including Korean, Chinese, Japanese, and Western cuisines. You'll find various categories including Kimchi Stew, Pork Belly, Jajangmyeon, Sushi, Pizza, Fried Chicken, and more.

Q. Can I use it on mobile?
Yes, What to Eat is optimized for mobile devices. You can conveniently use it on smartphones, tablets, and PCs.
